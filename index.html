About Me

As long as I can remember, I've always wanted to be a programmer. As I kid I spent a ton of time playing video games, and decided that when I grew up, I wanted to create games myself. It wasn't until my sophomore year at high school that I actually started programming in my AP Computer Science class.

After that, my programming life took off. For the next two years, I studied programming independently, until I got a job as an intern at Eleven Fifty Academy. There, I was introduced to web development, which helped broaden my skills. Currently, I attend Purdue University where I major in Computer Science, where I have taken an interest in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence.
